The project is all about online ticket booking.
Passengers book their tickets online through mobile app or desktop
Admin approves request of managers.
Managers insert buses and their routes.
Admin can delete and and add buses
Admin can delete and add managers 
Passenger can cancel booking
Passenger can pay through cash or online.
